Kyoto 5-Day Itinerary

A Journey Through Ancient Temples and Modern Marvels

Day 1 – Exploring Eastern Kyoto’s Temples

Eastern Kyoto offers the perfect introduction to the city’s spiritual and cultural heart. Begin your morning at Kiyomizu-dera, a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its massive wooden veranda that offers breathtaking views over the city. The temple’s history stretches back to the 8th century, and the walk through its halls gives you a sense of Kyoto’s timeless devotion to tradition. Afterward, stroll along Sannenzaka and Ninenzaka, two historic streets filled with preserved wooden townhouses, tea shops, and artisanal souvenir stores. Stop for a traditional sweet like yatsuhashi, a local cinnamon-flavored treat.

Day 2 – Arashiyama’s Natural Beauty

Escape to the western outskirts of Kyoto to discover the serene beauty of Arashiyama. Your day begins with a walk through the famous Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, where towering stalks of bamboo create an almost otherworldly atmosphere. The way the light filters through the dense forest is a truly magical experience. After the grove, visit the Tenryū-ji Temple, another World Heritage site known for its exquisite landscape garden that has survived the centuries in its original form.
